Toasted Brie and tomato sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 slices Brie
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 tbsp butter

Instructions

  • Spread butter onto one side of both bread slices.
  • Place the Brie slices onto the unbuttered side of one of the bread slices.
  • Arrange the tomato slices on top. Place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side facing upwards.
  • Heat a chargrill pan over a high heat, add the sandwich and fry for 2-3 minutes, or until golden-brown.
  • Carefully turn the sandwich, fry for a further 2-3 minutes, or until golden-brown, then transfer to a serving plate and serve immediately.
